TIME RUSHING

TimeRush is a web application designed to help you stay on track with your tasks. Whether you're a student managing assignments or an office worker juggling deadlines, TimeRush provides a structured approach to time management. The countdown timer keeps you focused while the performance reviews give insights on how you can be more productive!
